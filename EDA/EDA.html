Gapminder Data Analysis¶

Exploratory Data Analysis and Visualization with Python¶

by Nosheen Altaf

The DATA:¶

The present data is extracted from gapminder ,the data frames include six variables country, continent, year, lifeExp (life expectancy at birth), pop(total population), gdpPercap (per-capita GDP). Per-capita GDP (Gross domestic product) is given in units of international dollars, "a hypothetical unit of currency that has the same purchasing power parity that the U.S. dollar had in the United States at a given point in time"

Project Overview¶

we will explore the data by creating visualizations, summary statistics, distribution analysis etc.
